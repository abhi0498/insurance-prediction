THE DESCRIPTIVE STATISTICS OF THE GIVEN INSURANCE DATA

AGE

  • THE GIVEN DATA HAS A LOT OF PEOPLE OF DIFFERENT AGE GORUPS WHO HAVE APPLIED FOR INSURANCE
  • THE AGE GROUPS RANGES FROM 18-70 YEARS
  • THE CHARGES GOES ON INCREASING AS THE AGE INCREASES
  • THE AVERAGE AGE GROUP OF THE PEOPLE WHO HAVE APPLIED FOR INSURANCE ARE 39 AND ABOVE
  • MAXIMUM PROFIT CAN BE EARNED WHEN WE FOCUS MORE THE PEOPLE WHO ARE OF 64 YEARS OLD AND ABOVE

SEX

  • THERE ARE TWO CATEGORIES IN THE GIVEN DATA
  • THE CHARGES VARY FOR MALE AND FEMALE BASED ON THEIR NEEDS

BMI-BODY MASS INDEX

  • THE BMI IS DIFFERENT FOR DIFFERENT PEOPLE
  • BMI IS CALCULATED BASED THE PERSONS HEIGHT AND WEIGHT

CHILDREN

  • THE CHARGES VARY FOR PEOPLE DEPENDING ON NUMBER OF CHILDREN THEY HAVE

SMOKER

  • THE CHAGRES MAY VARY FOR A SMOKER AND A NON SMOKER
  • IF THEY HAVE A BIT OF SMOKING THE CHARGES ARE HIGH THAN THAT OF A NON-SMOKER

REGION

  • THERE ARE A TOTAL OF FOUR DIFFERENT REGIONS IN THE GIVEN DATA
  • THEY ARE NORTHEAST,NORTHWEST,SOUTHEAST AND SOUTHWEST
